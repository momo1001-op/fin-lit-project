My Money Story

Smart money habits to stay balanced and financially stable

Understanding the Mental Side of Money

Money can really affect how we feel. Stressing over bills or not having enough can lead to anxiety and poor decision-making. That’s why I created a simple system to stay on track — one that keeps me mentally healthy and financially sound.

My Monthly Budget Plan ($1,600/month)

  • Rent + Bills: $700 – A small, affordable place
  • Food: $250 – Groceries and occasional eating out
  • Transport: $100 – Gas or public transport
  • Savings: $300 – For emergencies and future needs
  • Fun/Extras: $150 – Entertainment, snacks, clothes
  • Phone/Internet: $100 – Essentials to stay connected

Habits That Keep Me Going

  • Budgeting: Writing down income and expenses brings control.
  • Saving: Even small amounts grow over time.
  • Mental Breaks: Music, walks, or games to reduce money anxiety.

Why It Works

Because it’s realistic. I keep expenses low, save regularly, and stay in control emotionally. When I feel stable mentally, I make better money choices and avoid falling into harmful spending habits or risky situations.
